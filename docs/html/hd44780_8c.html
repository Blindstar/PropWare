Initialize an HD44780 LCD display.

Parameters:
dataPinsMaskPin mask for all 4 or all 8 data wires; NOTE: all pins must be consecutive and the LSB on the LCD must be the LSB in your data mask (i.e., if you are using pins 16-23 on the Propeller, pin 16 must be connected to D0 on the LCD, NOT D7)
rs,rw,enPin masks for each of the RS, RW, and EN signals
bitmodeSelect between HD44780_4BIT and HD44780_8BIT modes to determine whether you will need 4 data wires or 8 between the Propeller and your LCD device
dimensionsDimensions of your LCD device. Most common is HD44780_16x2
